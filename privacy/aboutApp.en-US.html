About this application

  

Diatonic App Developed by: Flávio Vani
Musical coordination: Cezar Ferreira

This project is intended to be a reference map, both for learning and for keeping a historical record on diatonic accordions. By using this application is possible to configure - and to present - the layout of any diatonic accordion, in their most diverse models. The musical coordination was in charge of prof. Cezar Ferreira - Sala da Gaita Ponto (Facebook) , who provided the necessary material to create the layouts and the essential information on accordion tablatures, as well as songs and exercises to practice directly on the instrument.

In order to transform the project into a learning tool, we adopted a free library which allows you to edit sheet music in ABC format ( abcjs ) directly in the browser. Tha library has been extended to include a tone transposition tool and mainly we expanded the ABC ( non-standard ) notation to include a syntax for producing tablatures for accordions, merging the ABC model with the accordion maps presented here.

Finally, through the implementation of MIDI libraries for browsers ( MIDI.js ), it became possible to hear the sounds produced by the accordion, in the form of songs and exercises. This feature even works on mobile devices, such as Android phones. Many other libraries helped this project succeed, as shown below.

The ultimate goal is to provide a visual and interactive experience for those who are starting out their studies at "Diatonic Accordion".
